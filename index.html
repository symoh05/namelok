Our Bakery Story

Founded in 2015 in Ndumberi, Kiambu, Namelock Bakery has grown from a small home-based business to Kiambu's favorite artisan bakery. Our passion for quality ingredients and beautiful designs shines through in every product we create.

We specialize in custom cakes for weddings, birthdays, anniversaries and all special occasions. Our French-trained pastry chef creates exquisite desserts that taste as good as they look.

All our products are made fresh daily using locally-sourced ingredients whenever possible. We never use artificial preservatives or flavors.
